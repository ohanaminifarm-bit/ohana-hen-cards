The Small Print.
Plain & Simple.

We're a small family farm, not a corporation. These policies exist to protect both of us — and we've written them the same way we run the farm: honestly.

Returns, Refunds & Cancellations

Fresh food, real policies — fair on both ends.

Because we sell fresh food, all sales are final once items are picked up or delivered. That said, if something doesn't meet your expectations, please reach out within 24 hours — we're a small farm but we stand behind our products and will handle every concern fairly and thoughtfully.

CSA subscriptions are month-to-month. Cancellations are accepted 1 week prior to delivery.

Privacy Policy

Your info stays yours. Full stop.

We only collect what's necessary to communicate about orders, pickups, or subscriptions. Your details are never sold or shared — not to third parties, not to marketers, not to anyone.

We treat your information with the same care and respect we bring to everything on this farm.

Terms of Service

We're a living, breathing farm — not an Amazon warehouse.

By placing an order, you agree to our pricing, pickup or delivery arrangements, availability, and farm policies. Availability can — and does — change with seasons and livestock realities, and we genuinely appreciate your understanding when that happens.

We reserve the right to limit quantities or decline orders when needed. It's never personal — it's just farm life.

Food, Health & Safety Disclosures

Transparency is part of how we operate.

Our eggs and poultry are produced on a small family farm and sold directly to consumers under applicable state exemptions. Our products are not USDA-inspected. Safe handling and proper cooking are the responsibility of the customer.

  • Pasture-raised on our Oregon House property
  • Soy-free feed, non-GMO
  • Sold direct-to-consumer under CA state exemptions
  • Cook all poultry to safe internal temperatures

Why We Share This

Clear expectations build trust. These policies let us focus on what matters most — caring for the land, our animals, and the people who support our farm. When you know what to expect from us, we can show up for you the way Ohana is supposed to.

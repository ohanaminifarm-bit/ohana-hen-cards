One Egg at a Time.
One Smile at a Time.

At Ohana Mini Farm, our mission is to care for the land and birds while growing wholesome food that nourishes body and spirit. We cultivate joy, community, and the aloha spirit — one egg, one veggie, and one smile at a time.

What We Offer

Good Food, Raised Right

Everything here is raised slowly, thoughtfully, and close to home. When you buy from us, you're supporting humane animal care, better ingredients, and a food system built on trust — not volume.

What We Sell

  • Farm-fresh chicken eggs & seasonal duck eggs
  • Pasture-raised whole poultry (when available)
  • Seasonal farm goods — sunflowers & limited add-ons

How Pricing Works

  • Eggs sold by the dozen
  • Poultry sold per whole bird
  • CSA & preorders get priority access to limited items

Our prices reflect real costs: quality feed, good care, and small-batch production.

What's Included

  • Non-GMO, soy-free feed
  • Free-range, pasture-based care
  • Clear communication & transparency
  • Food raised with care from start to finish

What's Not Included

  • Custom cuts or individual parts
  • Unlimited supply year-round
  • Rush orders or last-minute guarantees

We keep things simple so we can do them well.

Availability & Limits

We Follow the Farm's Lead

Production is seasonal and naturally limited

Items offered first-come, first-served

Quantities vary with weather, flock health, and season

If something isn't available, it's because we're letting the land and animals set the pace — and that's exactly how it should be.

Who We Serve

Is This Farm Right for You?

This Is For You If…

  • You care about where your food comes from
  • Your family values local, responsibly raised food
  • You appreciate honesty, simplicity, and quality

Maybe Not If…

  • You're looking for the lowest possible price
  • You need unlimited customization or custom cuts
  • You expect grocery-store scale or convenience

And that's okay — different farms serve different needs.

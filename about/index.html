We're Not Just a Farm.
We're Ohana.

Ohana means family — and that's exactly what this is. A small plot of land, a whole lot of love, and a crew of people who show up every single day to do things right.

Why Ohana?

The Name Says Everything

Ohana is a Hawaiian word for family — but it means more than blood relation. It means pleasant & sociable. Humble & trustworthy. Gentle & kind. Precious & beautiful. Love & affection.

That's the standard we hold ourselves to on this farm — in how we treat our animals, our land, and every person we sell to. If you buy from us, you're part of it too.

The People

Meet the Ohana

Every farm runs on people. Here's ours.

Rocky & Phil

Founders · Heart & Muscle of the Farm

Rocky is the one who holds it all together — the planner, the processor, the one laughing in the kitchen while handling chickens most people wouldn't touch. She's the reason this farm has a voice and a standard.

Phil is the builder and the brawn. If there's a coop to frame, a fence to run, a tractor to drive, or a structure that needs to actually hold — that's Phil. He also happens to be the most patient chicken-feeding teacher a toddler could ask for.

Together they turned a piece of land in Oregon House into something real.

Bryce

Chief Egg Gatherer · Resident Adventurer

The newest and most enthusiastic member of the farm team. Bryce has rubber boots in every color, mud on every outfit, and the most serious approach to potato harvesting you've ever seen. He's learning that farms are built on daily work — and he's already pretty good at it.

Celine

Daughter · Farm Hand When Called

Phil and Rocky's daughter — she grew up watching this dream take shape and shows up when the farm needs an extra set of hands. Equal parts practical and warm, just like her parents.

Chris & Eryka

Son & Daughter-in-Law · Extra Farm Hands

When the work piles up or a big processing day rolls around, Chris and Eryka show up. They bring good energy, an extra pair of hands, and yes — a Sphynx cat who absolutely judges everyone from a distance.

April & Rich

Besties · Friends of the Farm

Not family by blood, but family by choice — which is what Ohana is all about. April and Rich show up, pitch in, and make every hard day easier. The kind of people every small farm needs in their corner.

Beyond the Farm

We're a Real Family

We work hard on this farm. We also go to Giants games, take the kids to Disneyland, and celebrate every chance we get. That's what Ohana means to us.

What We Stand For

Why We Do This

Honest Food

Non-GMO, soy-free feed. Pasture access. Clean housing. No shortcuts. We raise our animals the way we'd want our own family's food raised — because it is our family's food first.

Real People

When you reach out to us, you're talking to Rocky or Phil — not a system, not a chatbot, not someone reading from a script. We know our animals and we know our customers.

Built by Hand

Every coop, every pen, every structure on this property was built by Phil. This farm is a physical expression of what it means to commit to something — post by post, board by board.

Community First

Ohana means no one gets left behind. That includes the neighbors, customers, and community who support this farm. When you buy from us, you're keeping a local family doing meaningful work.

Come Say Hello

We love connecting with our community. Whether you want to visit the farm, ask about our animals, or just say hi — reach out. We respond to every message personally.
